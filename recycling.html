We can change the world and make it better place.

We can change the world for the better, and one way we can do that is by being mindful of our actions and their impact on the environment. Recycling is just one small action but powerful way that we can make a difference. By making a conscious effort to recycle, we can help reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect the planet for future generations.
Together, we can create a cleaner, greener world and make it a better place for everyone.

My Story ㅡ Think what you can do then Act Immediately

In order to do my part, I ensure that I separate my waste, use reusable bags, minimise food waste, recycle electronics, and support eco-friendly businesses, among other things.
However, I don't stop there. I am always on the lookout for other opportunities to inspire and encourage myself and others to adopt environmentally sustainable practices.
By actively engaging in such activities, I am doing my part to help protect the environment, and I hope to inspire others to do the same.

Trash2Flight

I turned "plastics&cans" into a flight ticket.


Every 400 Nectar points converted is 250 Avios

Some of Sainsbury's stores have a reverse vending recycling machine that allows customers to return and earn 5p coupons or 10 nectar points per bottle towards their shop.

A minimum of 400 Nectar points is necessary for converting them into Avios. Through the use of a reverse vending machine, I earned Nectar points, which were then converted to Avios points. This was achieved by diligently accumulating a sufficient number of points, enabling the purchase of my flight ticket to Paris.
